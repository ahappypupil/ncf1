💡 知识点
一般过去时与时间短语
📌 When + did + 主语 + 动词原形?
When did you look at that photograph?
When did she walk across the park?
回答：主语 + 动词过去式 + 时间短语
📌 过去时的时间标志
last night/week/month/year
the day before yesterday 前天
the week before last 上上周
a minute/an hour/two days ago
📌 一般过去时 vs 一般现在时
She walks to work every day. (现在习惯)
She walked to work yesterday. (过去动作)
💡 知识点
规则动词过去式
📌 加 -ed
walk → walked wash → washed
watch → watched listen → listened
paint → painted dust → dusted
air → aired cook → cooked
📌 发音规则
/t/ 声之后读 /ɪd/：wanted, needed
清辅音后读 /t/：walked, washed
浊辅音/元音后读 /d/：aired, listened
📝 核心句型
When did you + 动词原形?
你什么时候……？
📌 句型结构
When did you + 动词原形 + ...?
🔊 例句
When did you wash your hands?
I washed my hands a minute ago.
