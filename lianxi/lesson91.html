Has Ian sold his house yet?伊恩已经卖了他的房子吗？

👩
Has he moved to his new house yet?他已经搬进新房子了吗？
👩
No, not yet. He is still here. He is going to move tomorrow.还没有。他还在这里。他打算明天搬。
👩
When? Tomorrow morning?什么时候？明天上午？
👩
No, tomorrow afternoon. I will miss him. He has always been a good neighbour.不，明天下午。我会想念他的。他一直是个好邻居。
👩
He is a very nice person. We will all miss him.他是一个非常好的人。我们都会想念他的。
👩
When will the new people move into this house?新住户什么时候搬进这所房子？
💡 知识点
一般将来时：will + 动词原形
📌 结构
主语 + will + 动词原形
I will miss him. 我会想念他的。
We will all miss him. 我们都会想念他的。
He will move tomorrow. 他明天搬。
📌 否定句
I will not (won't) go. 我不会去。
She won't be late. 她不会迟到。
📌 疑问句
Will you go? 你会去吗？
When will they move? 他们什么时候搬？
💡 知识点
will vs be going to
📌 be going to
He is going to move tomorrow.
→ 计划好的、打算做的
I am going to buy a new car.
→ 已经决定的计划
📌 will
I will miss him.
→ 预测、意愿、临时决定
We will all miss him.
→ 较远的未来或不确定的计划
⚠️ 缩写
I will = I'll we will = we'll
he will = he'll they will = they'll
will not = won't
📝 核心句型
When will...?
什么时候会……？
📌 句型结构
When will + 主语 + 动词原形?
🔊 例句
When will the new people move in?
When will he come back?
