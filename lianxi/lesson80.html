对话场景
have got 与 much/many 练习 📉 点击句子可朗读
👧
Have you got any cheese?你有奶酪吗？
👦
I need a lot of cheese. I haven't got much.我需要很多奶酪。我没有多少了。
👧
I must go to the grocer's to get some cheese.我必须去杂货店买些奶酪。
👦
Has he got any envelopes?他有信封吗？
👧
He needs a lot of envelopes. He hasn't got many.他需要很多信封。他没有多少了。
👦
He must go to the newsagent's to get some envelopes.他必须去报刊亭买些信封。
👧
I haven't got any butter. I must go to the grocer's.我没有黄油了。我必须去杂货店。
👦
We haven't got much milk. We must get some.我们没有多少牛奶了。我们必须去买些。
💡 知识点
must 和 need 的用法
📌 must 表必须
I must go to the grocer's. 我必须去杂货店。
We must get some milk. 我们必须去买些牛奶。
⚠️ must 后接动词原形
📌 need 表需要
I need a lot of cheese. 我需要很多奶酪。
He needs some envelopes. 他需要一些信封。
Need I go? (情态动词) 我需要去吗？
📌 mustn't vs needn't
mustn't = 不准，禁止
needn't = 不需要，不必
You mustn't smoke here. 你不准在这里吸烟。
You needn't wait. 你不必等。
💡 知识点
have got 的各种句型
📌 肯定句
I have got some money. 我有一些钱。
She has got a lot of envelopes. 她有很多信封。
📌 否定句
I haven't got much cheese. 我没有多少奶酪。
He hasn't got many envelopes. 他没有多少信封。
📌 疑问句
Have you got any sugar? 你有糖吗？
Has she got any milk? 她有牛奶吗？
📝 核心句型
I must go to the... to get some...
我必须去……买些……
📌 句型结构
I must go to the + 商店 + to get some + 商品
🔊 例句
I must go to the grocer's to get some cheese.
He must go to the newsagent's to get some envelopes.
