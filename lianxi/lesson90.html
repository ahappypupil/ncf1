对话场景
for/since 时间表达 ⏰ 点击句子可朗读
👧
How long have you lived here?你在这里住了多久了？
👦
I have lived here for five years.我在这里住了5年了。
👧
How long has she worked here?她在这里工作多久了？
👦
She has worked here since 2010.她从2010年起就在这里工作了。
👧
How long have they known each other?他们认识多久了？
👦
They have known each other for a long time.他们认识很久了。
👧
Has he studied English for a long time?他学英语很久了吗？
👦
No, he has studied English since last year.不是，他从去年才开始学英语。

回答：for + 时间段 / since + 时间点
📌 更多例句
How long have you known him? 你认识他多久了？
I have known him for ten years. 我认识他10年了。
How long has she lived here? 她在这里住了多久？
She has lived here since 2015. 她从2015年起就住在这里。
💡 知识点
for 与 since 的区别
📌 for + 时间段
for 20 years 20年
for five months 5个月
for a long time 很久
for three weeks 3周
📌 since + 时间点
since 1976 从1976年起
since last year 从去年起
since yesterday 从昨天起
since this morning 从今天早上起
⚠️ 易错点
✗ I have lived here since 20 years.
✓ I have lived here for 20 years.
✗ I have lived here for 1976.
✓ I have lived here since 1976.
