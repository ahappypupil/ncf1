对话场景
在鞋店买鞋 👠 点击句子可朗读
👩
Do you have any shoes like these?你们有像这样的鞋吗？
👨
What size?多大尺码？
👩
Size five. Black.五号。黑色的。
👨
I am sorry. We do not have any.很抱歉。我们没有。
👩
But my sister bought this pair last month.但我姐姐上个月买了这双。
👨
Did she buy them here?她是在这儿买的吗？
👩
No, she bought them in the U.S.不是，她是在美国买的。
👨
They were in fashion last year. But they are not in fashion this year.它们去年流行。但今年不流行了。
💡 知识点
buy 的过去式：bought
📌 bought 的用法
My sister bought this pair last month.
我姐姐上个月买了这双。
Did she buy them here? 她是在这儿买的吗？
⚠️ 疑问句/否定句用 did + buy，动词用原形
📌 其他不规则动词
wear → wore buy → bought
bring → brought think → thought
catch → caught teach → taught
💡 知识点
in fashion / a pair of
📌 in fashion
in fashion = 流行中
not in fashion = 不流行了
These shoes are in fashion now. 这些鞋现在流行。
📌 a pair of
a pair of shoes 一双鞋
a pair of socks 一双袜子
a pair of trousers 一条裤子
