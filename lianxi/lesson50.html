💡 知识点
第三人称单数的肯定和否定
📌 肯定句
He/She likes tomatoes. 他/她喜欢番茄。
He/She likes meat. 他/她喜欢肉。
📌 否定句
He/She doesn't like cabbage. 他/她不喜欢卷心菜。
He/She doesn't like peas. 他/她不喜欢豌豆。
⚠️ 动词变化规则
一般加 s: like → likes
以s/x/ch/sh/o结尾加 es: go → goes
辅音+y → 变y为i加 es: study → studies
💡 知识点
Does he/she like...?
📌 疑问句
Does he/she like + 名词?
Does he like tomatoes?
Does she like cabbage?
🔊 肯定回答

🔊 否定回答
No, he/she doesn't.
