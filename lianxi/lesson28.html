💡 知识点
现在进行时的复数形式
📌 单数 vs 复数
What is he/she doing?
He/She is V-ing.
What are they doing?
They are V-ing.
🔊 例句
She's typing a letter. → They're typing letters.
He's cooking a meal. → They're cooking a meal.
⚠️ 注意
They're = They are
他们/她们/它们 都用 They're
💡 知识点
V-ing 双写规则
📌 什么时候双写？
以一个辅音字母结尾的重读闭音节
双写末尾辅音字母再加 -ing
🔊 双写例子
run → running
sit → sitting
swim → swimming
sweep → sweeping
🔊 不双写例子
read → reading
cook → cooking
type → typing（以e结尾去e）
write → writing
📝 核心句型
What are they doing? / They're V-ing
他们在做什么？/ 他们正在……
📌 句型结构
What are they doing?
They're + V-ing + 宾语.
🔊 例句
They're typing letters.
They're cooking a meal.
They're cleaning the room.
They're washing dishes.
🎯 do 和 doing
do homework → doing homework
do housework → doing housework
do 的现在分词：doing
