💡 知识点
一般现在时的各种句式
📌 肯定句
I/You/We/They eat lunch at noon.
He/She eats lunch at noon.
📌 否定句
I/You/We/They don't eat lunch at noon.
He/She doesn't eat lunch at noon.
📌 疑问句
Do you eat lunch at noon?
Does he/she eat lunch at noon?
💡 知识点
频率副词的排序
📌 从高到低
always (100%) 总是
usually (80%) 通常
often (60%) 经常
sometimes (30%) 有时
never (0%) 从不
🔊 例句
She always eats her lunch at noon.
She usually sees her friends.
She often drinks tea with them.
She sometimes goes to the shops.
She never arrives home late.
📝 核心句型
What does he/she usually do?
他/她通常做什么？
📌 句型结构

What do you/they usually do?
🔊 例句

She usually stays at home.
