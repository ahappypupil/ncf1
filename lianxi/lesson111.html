💡 知识点
as...as 同级比较
📌 as + 形容词原级 + as
It's as good as the expensive one. 它和那个贵的一样好。
She is as tall as her sister. 她和她姐姐一样高。
📌 not as/so + 形容词原级 + as
It's not as good as the expensive one. 它没有那个贵的好。
He is not as old as I am. 他没有我大。
⚠️ not as...as = 比较级 + than
It's not as good as the expensive one.
= The expensive one is better than this one.
💡 知识点
cost 的用法
📌 cost 作动词
It costs five hundred pounds. 它值五百英镑。
How much does it cost? 多少钱？
⚠️ cost 的过去式还是 cost（三态同形）
📌 cost vs price
cost（动词/名词）：花费/费用「,」price（名词）：价格，标价
The price is too high. 价格太高了。
It costs too much. 它花费太多了。
📝 核心句型
It's not as ... as ...
它没有……那么……
📌 句型结构
It's not as + adj. + as + 比较对象
= 比较对象 + is + 比较级 + than + it.
🔊 例句
It's not as good as the expensive one.
It's not as expensive as that model.
