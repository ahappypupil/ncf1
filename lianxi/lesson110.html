数量与程度比较 📊 点击句子可朗读
👧
I have more coffee than you have.我比你咖啡多。
👦
I have less tea than you have.我比你茶少。
👧
There are more books on this shelf.这个书架上的书更多。
👦
There are fewer books on that shelf.那个书架上的书更少。
👧
She is more beautiful than her sister.她比她妹妹更漂亮。
👦
This is the most beautiful dress in the shop.这是店里最漂亮的裙子。
👧
He is better at English than I am.他英语比我好。
👦
She is the best student in the class.她是班上最好的学生。
💡 知识点
多音节词的比较级和最高级
📌 more + 多音节词 → 比较级
beautiful → more beautiful → most beautiful
expensive → more expensive → most expensive
difficult → more difficult → most difficult
📌 数量比较
more + 不可数名词 → 更多的
less + 不可数名词 → 更少的
more + 可数名词复数 → 更多的
fewer + 可数名词复数 → 更少的
💡 知识点
不规则比较级与最高级总表
📌 完整对照
good → better → best
bad → worse → worst
much/many → more → most
little → less → least
far → farther → farthest
📌 fewer vs less
fewer + 可数名词复数：fewer books
less + 不可数名词：less water/coffee
⚠️ 不能混用！
📝 核心句型
I have more ... than ...
我比……有更多的……
📌 句型结构
more/fewer/less + 名词 + than
I have more coffee than you have.
🔊 例句
I have more coffee than you have.
I have less tea than you have.
There are fewer books on that shelf.
