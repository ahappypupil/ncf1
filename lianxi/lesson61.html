💡 知识点
What's the matter with...?
📌 句型结构
What's the matter with + 人?
= What's wrong with...?
用来询问某人怎么了
🔊 回答
He feels ill. 他觉得不舒服。
He has a bad cold. 他得了重感冒。
He has a headache. 他头痛。
⚠️ have got = have
He has a cold. = He has got a cold.
She has a fever. = She has got a fever.
💡 知识点
must 的用法
📌 表示必须
must + 动词原形 → 必须……
He must stay in bed. 他必须卧床。
We must call the doctor. 我们必须请医生。
🔊 mustn't = must not
You mustn't eat rich food. 你不能吃油腻食物。
You mustn't get up yet. 你还不能起床。
⚠️ 注意
must 后面用动词原形
must 没有人称变化
He must stay. ✅ He must stays. ❌
📝 核心句型
主语 + must + 动词原形
必须……
📌 句型结构
must + 动词原形
He must stay in bed.
🔊 例句
He must stay in bed for a week.
We must call the doctor.
You must take some medicine.
