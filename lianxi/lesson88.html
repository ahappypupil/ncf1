💡 知识点
Have you... yet?
📌 疑问句
Have you finished your homework yet?
Has she left yet?
Have they found the car yet?
⚠️ yet 放在句末
📌 肯定回答
Yes, I have already finished it.
Yes, she has already left.
⚠️ 肯定回答用 already
📌 否定回答
No, I haven't finished it yet.
No, she hasn't left yet.
⚠️ 否定回答用 yet
💡 知识点
更多过去分词
📌 过去式 = 过去分词
leave → left → left
lose → lost → lost
hear → heard → heard
build → built → built
sell → sold → sold
📌 过去式 ≠ 过去分词
fly → flew → flown
write → wrote → written
speak → spoke → spoken
eat → ate → eaten
see → saw → seen
📝 核心句型
