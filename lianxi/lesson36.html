对话场景
询问人们去哪里 🗺️ 点击句子可朗读
👦
Where are you going?你去哪里？
👧
I'm going home.我回家。
👦
Where is she going?她去哪里？
👧
She's going to the shop.她去商店。
👦
Where are they sitting?他们坐在哪里？
👧
They're sitting beside the river.他们坐在河边。
👦
Where is he swimming?他在哪里游泳？
👧
He's swimming across the river.他在游过河。
👦
Where are they walking?他们在哪里走？
👧
They're walking along the street.他们在沿着街道走。
👦
Where are the children going?孩子们去哪里？
👧
They're going into the park.他们正走进公园。
💡 知识点
Where 的综合用法
📌 Where + be + 人 + going?
询问某人去哪里
回答用 be going to + 地点
📌 Where + be + 人 + V-ing?
询问某人在哪里做某事
回答用 be + V-ing + 介词短语
🔊 介词短语总结
across the river 穿过河
along the street 沿着街道
beside the river 在河边
into the park 进入公园
out of the building 从大楼出来
💡 知识点
go to + 地点
📌 规则
go to + 地点名词
go home （home前不加to）
🔊 例句
go to school 🏫 去上学
go to the shop 🏪 去商店
go home 🏠 回家（不加to！）
⚠️ 特殊情况
go home ✅（home是副词，不加to）
go to the home ❌
📝 核心句型
Where are you going? / I'm going to...
你去哪里？/ 我去……
📌 句型结构
Where are you going?
I'm going to + 地点 / I'm going home.
🔊 例句
Where is she going? → She's going to the shop.
Where are they going? → They're going into the park.
