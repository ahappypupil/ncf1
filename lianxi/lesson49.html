💡 知识点
一般现在时的肯定和否定
📌 肯定句
I/You/We/They like meat.
He/She likes meat.（第三人称加s）
📌 否定句
I/You/We/They don't like meat.
He/She doesn't like meat.
don't = do not, doesn't = does not
⚠️ 注意
He likes. ✅ He doesn't like. ✅
He doesn't likes. ❌（doesn't后用原形）
He not likes. ❌
💡 知识点
Do you want... or...? 选择疑问句
📌 句型结构
Do you want A or B?
你想要A还是B？
回答要具体选择，不用Yes/No
🔊 例句
Do you want beef or lamb?
Beef, please. 请给我牛肉。
🔊 What about...? 怎么样？
What about some steak?
来点牛排怎么样？
用于提出建议
📝 核心句型
Do you want + A or B?
你想要A还是B？
📌 句型结构
Do you want + 名词A + or + 名词B?
🔊 例句
Do you want beef or lamb?
Do you want chicken or steak?
Do you want tea or coffee?
