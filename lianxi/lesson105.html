💡 知识点
动词不定式作宾语补足语
📌 want sb. to do sth.
I want her to come to my office. 我要她来我的办公室。
I want you to type this letter again. 我要你把这封信再打一遍。
⚠️ to 不能省略
📌 tell sb. to do sth.
Tell her to come at once. 叫她马上来。
Tell him to wait. 叫他等着。
📌 tell sb. not to do sth.
Tell him not to wait. 叫他别等了。
Tell her not to be late. 叫她别迟到。
💡 知识点
be full of 的用法
📌 be full of = be filled with
This letter is full of mistakes. 这封信错误百出。
The glass is full of water. 杯子里装满了水。
The room is full of people. 房间里挤满了人。
📌 make a mistake / make mistakes
Everyone can make a mistake. 人人都可能犯错误。
Don't make the same mistakes. 不要犯同样的错误。

I want + sb. + to + 动词原形
Tell + sb. + to + 动词原形
