💡 知识点
Where is / Where are 的用法
📌 单数 vs 复数
Where is the cup? → It's on the table.
Where are the cups? → They're on the table.
🔊 缩写形式
Where is = Where's
It is = It's
Where are 没有缩写
They are = They're
🎯 回答方式
It's + 方位介词 + the + 名词.
They're + 方位介词 + the + 名词.
💡 知识点
方位介词的总结
📌 常用介词
in → 在……里面
on → 在……上面
under → 在……下面
near → 在……附近
🔊 更多介词
behind → 在……后面
in front of → 在……前面
between → 在……之间
next to → 在……旁边
🎯 介词短语的位置
放在句末：It's on the table.
放在名词后：the book on the desk
📝 核心句型
Where is...? / It's in/on/under...
……在哪里？/ 它在……
📌 句型结构
Where is the + 名词单数?
It's + 介词 + the + 名词.
🔊 例句
Where is the cup? → It's on the table.
Where are the knives? → They're on the plate.
✅ 单复数对比
单：Where is the box? → It's under the table.
复：Where are the boxes? → They're under the table.
