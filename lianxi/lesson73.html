💡 知识点
不规则动词过去式
📌 常见不规则动词
go → went see → saw
speak → spoke understand → understood
take → took read → read(/rɛd/)
say → said do → did
📌 课文中的例句
She went to London. 她去了伦敦。
She saw a man near a bus stop. 她在公交站旁看到一个男人。
He spoke German. 他讲德语。
💡 知识点
副词的构成
📌 形容词 + ly → 副词
pleasant → pleasantly 愉快地
slow → slowly 缓慢地
sudden → suddenly 突然地
hurried → hurriedly 匆忙地
⚠️ 特殊变化
以 -y 结尾：变 y 为 i 加 -ly
happy → happily thirsty → thirstily
📌 形容词与副词同形
fast 快的/快地 hard 努力的/努力地
late 迟的/迟地 well 好的/好地
📝 核心句型
Can you tell me the way to...?
您能告诉我去……怎么走吗？
📌 句型结构
Can you tell me the way to + 地点?
回答：Go along... / Turn left/right...
🔊 例句
Can you tell me the way to King Street?
Can you tell me the way to the station?
