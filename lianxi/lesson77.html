💡 知识点
have 表生病
📌 have + 疾病名词
I have a terrible toothache. 我牙痛得要命。
She has a headache. 她头痛。
He has a stomachache. 他胃痛。
⚠️ 注意：have 表生病时 不用进行时
📌 常见病痛表达
have a toothache 牙痛
have a headache 头痛
have a stomachache 胃痛
have a temperature 发烧
💡 知识点
情态动词 can / must
📌 can 的用法
Can you come at 10 a.m.? 你能在上午10点来吗？
I can wait. 我可以等。
I cannot (can't) wait. 我不能等。
📌 must 的用法
I must see the dentist now. 我必须现在看牙医。
⚠️ must 表示 必须、一定，语气比 can 强
📌 can't / must 表推测
That can't be true. 那不可能是真的。
You must be tired. 你一定累了。
📝 核心句型
Can you come at...?
您能在……来吗？
📌 句型结构
Can you come at + 时间?
🔊 例句
Can you come at 10 a.m. on Monday?
Can you come at 2 p.m.?
