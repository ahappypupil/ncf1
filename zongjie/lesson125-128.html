第125-128课 情态动词must与have to

Lesson 125-128 | 核心句型：喝茶、一个著名的女演员 | 语法点：情态动词must的用法、have to的用法、must与have to的区别、need的用法

核心句型

1. 第125-126课 喝茶

Susan: Can't you come in and have tea now, Peter? 苏珊：彼得，你现在能进来喝茶吗？
Peter: Not yet. I must water the garden first. 彼得：还不能。我得先给花园浇水。
Susan: Do you have to water it now? 苏珊：你必须现在浇吗？
Peter: I'm afraid I must. Look at it! It's terribly dry. 彼得：恐怕是的。你看这花园，干得厉害。
Susan: What a nuisance! 苏珊：真讨厌！
Peter: Last summer it was very dry, too. Don't you remember? I had to water it every day. 彼得：去年夏天也是干得很。你不记得了吗？我不得不每天都浇水。
Susan: Well, I'll have tea by myself. 苏珊：好吧，我一个人喝茶了。
Susan: That was quick! Have you finished already? 苏珊：好快啊！你已经浇完了？
Peter: Yes. Look out of the window. 彼得：是的。你看看窗外。
Susan: It's raining! That means you don't need to water the garden. 苏珊：下雨了！这就是说，你不必给花园浇水了。
Peter: That was a pleasant surprise. It means I can have tea, instead. 彼得：这真是意想不到的好事。这就是说我可以喝茶了。

2. 第127-128课 一个著名的女演员

Karen: Can you recognize that woman, Liz? 卡伦：莉兹，你能认出那个女人吗？
Liz: I think I can, Karen. It must be Karen Marsh, the actress. 莉兹：我想我认得出来，卡伦。那一定是女演员卡伦·马什。
Karen: I thought so. Who's that beside her? 卡伦：我也这么想。她旁边的那个人是谁？
Liz: That must be Conrad Reeves. 莉兹：那一定是康拉德·里弗斯。
Karen: Conrad Reeves, the actor? It can't be. Let me have another look. 卡伦：康拉德·里弗斯，那个男演员？不可能。让我再看看。
I think you're right! Isn't he her third husband? 我想你是对的！他不是她的第三任丈夫吗？
Liz: No. He must be her fourth or fifth. 莉兹：不。他一定是她的第四任或第五任丈夫。
Karen: Doesn't Karen Marsh look old! 卡伦：卡伦·马什看上去可真老啊！
Liz: She does, doesn't she! I read she's twenty-nine, but she must be at least forty. 莉兹：是啊，可不是吗！我在报上看到她是29岁，但她一定至少有40岁了。
Karen: I'm sure she is. 卡伦：我肯定她有40岁了。
Liz: She was a famous actress when I was still at school. 莉兹：我还在上学时，她就是个著名的女演员了。
Karen: That was a long time ago, wasn't it? 卡伦：那是很久以前的事了，不是吗？
Liz: Not that long ago! I'm not more than twenty-nine myself. 莉兹：没有那么久！我自己现在还没29岁呢。

语法知识点

1. 情态动词must的用法

must是情态动词，没有人称和数的变化，后面接动词原形。

表示"必须，应该"（表示义务或命令）：

You must finish your homework before you watch TV. 你必须先完成作业才能看电视。
We must obey the traffic rules. 我们必须遵守交通规则。
I must go now, or I'll be late. 我现在必须走了，否则就要迟到了。

表示"肯定，一定"（表示推测，用于肯定句）：

You have worked hard all day. You must be tired. 你辛苦工作了一整天，一定累了。
That man must be our new teacher. I saw him at the school gate this morning. 那个人一定是我们的新老师，我今天早上在学校门口看到他了。
She has lived in Paris for ten years. She must speak French very well. 她在巴黎住了十年，法语一定说得很好。

must的否定形式：

mustn't 表示"禁止，不允许"：You mustn't smoke here. 你不许在这里吸烟。
表示"不必"用needn't或don't have to，不用mustn't：You needn't come to school tomorrow if you are ill. 如果你生病了，明天不必来上学。

2. have to的用法

have to表示"必须，不得不"，有人称和时态的变化。

一般现在时：

I have to get up early every morning. 我每天早上都得早起。
He has to work on weekends. 他周末得上班。

一般过去时：

I had to walk home because I missed the last bus. 我错过了末班车，不得不走路回家。
She had to stay at home to look after her sick mother yesterday. 她昨天不得不呆在家里照顾生病的母亲。

一般将来时：

We will have to finish this work next week. 我们下周得完成这项工作。
You will have to study harder if you want to pass the exam. 如果你想通过考试，就得更加努力学习。

否定句和疑问句：

I don't have to go to work on Sundays. 我星期天不用上班。
He doesn't have to get up early on weekends. 他周末不用早起。
Do you have to leave now? 你现在必须走吗？
Does she have to go to the meeting? 她必须去开会吗？

3. must与have to的区别

区别 must have to
语气来源 表示说话人的主观意愿或命令 表示客观的需要，有"不得不"的意味
人称变化 没有人称和数的变化 有人称、数和时态的变化
时态 只有现在时形式 有现在、过去、将来等多种时态
否定含义 mustn't表示"禁止，不允许" don't have to表示"不必"

例句对比：

I must go now. 我现在必须走了。（主观上想走）
I have to go now. My mother is waiting for me. 我现在不得不走了，我妈妈在等我。（客观原因使得不得不走）
You mustn't tell him about it. 你不许告诉他这件事。（禁止）
You don't have to tell him about it. 你不必告诉他这件事。（没有必要）

4. 情态动词表示推测的用法

情态动词 用法 例句
must 用于肯定句，表示很有把握的推测"一定" He knows a lot about computers. He must be a computer engineer. 他对电脑懂得很多，一定是个电脑工程师。
can't 用于否定句，表示很有把握的否定推测"不可能" That man can't be Mr. Li. Mr. Li has gone to Beijing. 那个人不可能是李先生，李先生去北京了。
may/might 表示不太有把握的推测"可能，也许" It may rain this afternoon. You'd better take an umbrella. 今天下午可能下雨，你最好带把伞。

对现在情况的推测：情态动词 + 动词原形

She looks pale. She must be ill. 她脸色苍白，一定是病了。
He can't be at home. I saw him in the office just now. 他不可能在家，我刚才还在办公室看见他了。

对正在进行的动作的推测：情态动词 + be + 现在分词

The lights are on. They must be working now. 灯亮着，他们现在一定在工作。
He can't be sleeping. It's only 8 o'clock. 他不可能在睡觉，现在才8点。

5. need的用法

need作情态动词：

多用于否定句和疑问句，没有人称和时态的变化，后面接动词原形。

Need I come tomorrow? 我明天需要来吗？
You needn't worry about him. He is old enough to take care of himself. 你不必为他担心，他已经长大了，能照顾自己了。

need作实义动词：

可以用于各种句式，有人称和时态的变化，后面接名词、动词不定式或动名词。

I need a new pen. 我需要一支新钢笔。
He needs to see a doctor. 他需要看医生。
The room needs cleaning. = The room needs to be cleaned. 房间需要打扫了。
Do you need any help? 你需要帮忙吗？
She doesn't need to go there alone. 她不需要一个人去那里。

配套练习

练习一：用must或have to的适当形式填空

1. I ______ go to bed early tonight because I have an exam tomorrow.

2. She ______ stay at home to look after her little sister yesterday.

3. You ______ be tired after the long journey.

4. He ______ work hard to pass the exam.

5. We ______ follow the school rules.

1. must/have to 2. had to 3. must 4. has to/must 5. must/have to

练习二：用must, can't, may填空

1. He has studied English for many years. He ______ speak English very well.

2. That ______ be Mr. Wang. He has gone to America.

3. It ______ rain tomorrow, so you'd better take an umbrella.

4. She is not here. She ______ be in the library.

5. You have worked all day. You ______ be very tired.

1. must 2. can't 3. may 4. may/must 5. must

练习三：选择填空

1. You ______ talk loudly in the library.
A. mustn't B. don't have to C. needn't

2. He ______ go now, because his mother is waiting for him.
A. must B. have to C. has to

3. ______ I finish the work today? No, you needn't.
A. Must B. Have to C. Can

4. That man ______ be our teacher. Our teacher is much taller.
A. must B. can't C. may

5. You ______ worry. Everything will be all right.
A. mustn't B. needn't C. can't

1. A 2. C 3. A 4. B 5. B

练习四：改写句子

1. I have to get up early every morning.（改为否定句）

2. She has to finish her homework before 8 o'clock.（改为一般疑问句）

3. You must come here before 9 o'clock.（改为否定句，表示"不必"）

4. He must be sleeping now.（改为否定句，表示"不可能"）

5. We had to stay at home last night because of the bad weather.（改为一般疑问句）

1. I don't have to get up early every morning.

2. Does she have to finish her homework before 8 o'clock?

3. You needn't come here before 9 o'clock. / You don't have to come here before 9 o'clock.

4. He can't be sleeping now.

5. Did you have to stay at home last night because of the bad weather?

练习五：翻译下列句子

2. 他昨天不得不呆在家里照顾生病的母亲。

3. 你一定是饿了，吃点蛋糕吧。

4. 你不必担心，我能照顾好自己。

5. 那不可能是玛丽，她现在在上海。

1. I must leave now. / I have to leave now.

2. He had to stay at home to look after his sick mother yesterday.

3. You must be hungry. Have some cake.

4. You needn't worry. I can take good care of myself.
