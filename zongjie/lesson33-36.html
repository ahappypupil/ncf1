第33-36课 天气与现在进行时

Lesson 33-36 | 核心句型：天气描述、现在进行时扩展 | 语法点：现在进行时、方向介词、动词短语

核心句型

1. 第33-34课 描述天气

It is a fine day today. 今天天气好。
There are some clouds in the sky, but the sun is shining. 天空中飘着几朵云，但阳光灿烂。
Mr. Jones is with his family. 琼斯先生同他的家人在一起。
They are walking over the bridge. 他们正在过桥。
There are some boats on the river. 河上有几艘船。
Mr. Jones and his wife are looking at them. 琼斯先生和他的妻子正在看这些船。
Sally is looking at a big ship. 萨莉正在看一艘大船。
The ship is going under the bridge. 那船正从桥下驶过。
Tim is looking at an aeroplane. 蒂姆正望着一架飞机。
The aeroplane is flying over the river. 飞机正从河上飞过。
The sun is shining. 太阳在照耀着。
The children are jumping off the wall. 孩子们正从墙上跳下来。

2. 第35-36课 村庄照片

This is a photograph of our village. 这是我们村庄的一张照片。
Our village is in a valley. 我们的村庄坐落在一个山谷之中。
It is between two hills. 它位于两座小山之间。
The village is on a river. 它靠近一条小河。
Here is another photograph of the village. 这是我们村庄的另一张照片。
My wife and I are walking along the banks of the river. 我和妻子正沿着河岸走。
We are on the left. 我们在左边。
There is a boy in the water. 水里有个男孩。
He is swimming across the river. 他正横渡小河。
Here is another photograph. 这是另一张照片。
This is the school building. 这是学校大楼。
It is beside a park. 它位于公园的旁边。
The park is on the right. 公园在右边。
Some children are coming out of the building. 一些孩子正从楼里出来。
Some of them are going into the park. 他们中有几个正走进公园。

语法知识点

1. 天气描述词汇

fine

晴朗的

cloudy

多云的

sunny

阳光充足的

rainy

下雨的

windy

有风的

snowy

下雪的

cold

寒冷的

hot

炎热的

天气表达常用句型：

It is + 形容词 + today. 今天天气...
It is fine today. 今天天气晴朗。
It is cold today. 今天很冷。
There is + 名词 + in the sky. 天空中有...
There are some clouds in the sky. 天空中有一些云。

2. 方向介词

表示动作方向的介词，常与动词搭配使用。

介词 含义 例句
over 越过，在...上方 The plane is flying over the mountain. 飞机正在飞越山峰。
under 在...下面 The ship is going under the bridge. 船正从桥下驶过。
across 横过，穿过 He is swimming across the river. 他正游过河。
along 沿着 We are walking along the street. 我们正沿着街道走。
off 从...离开 The children are jumping off the wall. 孩子们正从墙上跳下来。
into 进入 They are going into the park. 他们正走进公园。
out of 从...出来 He is coming out of the room. 他正从房间里出来。
beside 在...旁边 The school is beside the park. 学校在公园旁边。

3. 现在进行时的特殊疑问句

对动作进行提问时使用现在进行时的特殊疑问句。

（1）对"做什么"提问：What + be + 主语 + doing?

What are you doing? 你在做什么？
I am reading a book. 我在看书。
What is he doing? 他在做什么？
He is playing football. 他在踢足球。

（2）对"谁"提问：Who + be + 动词-ing + 其他？

Who is singing in the room? 谁在房间里唱歌？
Mary is singing in the room. 玛丽在房间里唱歌。

（3）对"在哪里"提问：Where + be + 主语 + 动词-ing？

Where are they playing basketball? 他们在哪里打篮球？
They are playing basketball on the playground. 他们在操场上打篮球。

4. 常用动词短语搭配

动词 + 介词 含义 例句
walk over 走过 They are walking over the bridge. 他们正在过桥。
look at 看 She is looking at the picture. 她正在看图片。
go under 从下面经过 The train is going under the tunnel. 火车正从隧道下驶过。
fly over 飞过 The bird is flying over the house. 鸟儿正从房子上空飞过。
jump off 从...跳下 The cat is jumping off the table. 猫正从桌上跳下来。
walk along 沿着...走 They are walking along the river. 他们正沿着河边走。
swim across 游过 He is swimming across the pool. 他正游过游泳池。
come out of 从...出来 She is coming out of the shop. 她正从商店里出来。
go into 进入 We are going into the cinema. 我们正走进电影院。

配套练习

练习一：选择合适的介词填空

1. The plane is flying ______ the river. (over / under)

over

2. They are walking ______ the street. (along / across)

along

3. He is swimming ______ the river. (across / off)

across

练习二：将下列句子改为现在进行时

1. He swims across the river.

He is swimming across the river.

2. We walk along the street.

We are walking along the street.

练习三：对划线部分提问

1. I am reading a book. （对reading a book提问）

What are you doing?

2. They are playing football on the playground. （对on the playground提问）

Where are they playing football?

练习四：翻译下列句子

1. 今天天气晴朗。

It is a fine day today.

2. 孩子们正从教室里出来。

The children are coming out of the classroom.

3. 他们正走过桥。

They are walking over the bridge.
