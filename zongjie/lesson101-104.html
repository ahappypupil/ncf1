第101-104课 直接引语与间接引语

核心句型

1. 第101-102课 寄明信片

Grandma: Read Jimmy's card to me please, Penny. 奶奶：请把吉米的明信片念给我听听，彭妮。
Penny: "I have just arrived in Scotland and I'm staying at a Youth Hostel." 彭妮："我刚到苏格兰，我现住在一家青年招待所。"
Grandma: Eh? 奶奶：什么？
Penny: He says he's just arrived in Scotland. He says he's staying at a Youth Hostel. 彭妮：他说他刚到苏格兰。他说他住在一家青年招待所。
You know he's a member of the Y.H.A. 你知道，他是"青招协"的成员。
Grandma: What else does he say? 奶奶：他还说了些什么？
Penny: "I'll write a letter soon. I hope you are all well." 彭妮："我很快会写信的。祝你们大家身体都好。"
Grandma: What? Speak up, Penny. I'm afraid I can't hear you. 奶奶：什么？彭妮，大声一点。我可听不见你念的。
Penny: He says he'll write a letter soon. He hopes we are all well. 彭妮：他说他很快会写信的。他祝我们大家身体好。
Grandma: Is that all? 奶奶：就这些吗？
Penny: He doesn't say very much, does he? 彭妮：他没写很多，是吗？
Grandma: He can't write very much on a card, mother. 奶奶：在明信片上他写不了多少内容，妈妈。

2. 第103-104课 法语考试

Gary: How was the exam, Richard? 加里：考试考得怎么样，理查德？
Richard: Not too bad. I think I passed in English and Mathematics. 理查德：不算太坏，我想我的英语和数学及格了。
The questions were very easy. How about you, Gary? 题目很容易。加里，你怎么样？
Gary: The English and Maths papers weren't easy enough for me. 加里：英语和数学试题对我来说不那么容易。
I hope I haven't failed. 我希望别不及格。
Richard: I think I failed the French paper. 理查德：我想我的法语不及格。
I could answer sixteen of the questions. They were very easy. 我能回答其中的16道题。这些题很容易。
But I couldn't answer the rest. They were too difficult for me. 但我回答不出其余的题。那些题对我来说太难了。
Gary: French tests are awful, aren't they? 加里：法语测试太讨厌了，不是吗？
Richard: I hate them. I'm sure I've got a low mark. 理查德：我讨厌法语考试。我相信我的分数肯定很低。
Gary: Oh, cheer up! Perhaps we didn't do too badly. 加里：啊，别灰心！或许我们考得还不太糟。
The guy next to me wrote his name at the top of the paper. 坐在我旁边的那个人只在试卷顶端写了自己的名字。
Richard: Yes? 理查德：是吗？
Gary: Then he sat there and looked at it for three hours! He didn't write a word! 加里：然后他就坐在那里，对着考卷看了3个小时，一个字也没写！

语法知识点

1. 直接引语与间接引语概念

直接引语就是直接引用别人的话，放在引号里；间接引语就是用自己的话转述别人的话，不放在引号里，通常构成宾语从句。

直接引语：

Jimmy said: "I have just arrived in Scotland." 吉米说："我刚到苏格兰。"
（直接引用吉米的原话，放在引号内）

间接引语：

Jimmy said that he had just arrived in Scotland. 吉米说他刚到苏格兰。
（转述吉米的话，没有引号，由that引导宾语从句）

2. 直接引语变间接引语的时态变化

当主句的谓语动词是一般过去时（said, told等），间接引语的时态要相应地向后推移：

直接引语时态 间接引语时态 例句
一般现在时 一般过去时 He said: "I am happy." → He said that he was happy.
现在进行时 过去进行时 He said: "I am working." → He said that he was working.
现在完成时 过去完成时 He said: "I have finished." → He said that he had finished.
一般过去时 过去完成时 He said: "I bought a car." → He said that he had bought a car.
一般将来时 过去将来时 He said: "I will go." → He said that he would go.
过去完成时 过去完成时（不变） He said: "I had finished before 5 o'clock." → He said that he had finished before 5 o'clock.

特殊情况：

① 如果直接引语是客观真理、谚语等，时态不变
The teacher said: "The earth goes around the sun." → The teacher said that the earth goes around the sun.
② 如果主句是一般现在时，时态不变
He says: "I am happy." → He says that he is happy.

3. 直接引语变间接引语的人称变化

人称变化遵循"一随主，二随宾，第三人称不更新"的规则：

一随主：直接引语中的第一人称随主句的主语变化

He said: "I like English." → He said that he liked English.
I said: "I will go." → I said that I would go.

二随宾：直接引语中的第二人称随主句的宾语变化

He said to me: "You should study hard." → He told me that I should study hard.
She said to Tom: "You are right." → She told Tom that he was right.

第三人称不更新：直接引语中的第三人称不需要变化

He said: "She is a student." → He said that she was a student.
They said: "They will come." → They said that they would come.

5. too和enough的用法

too的用法：太……（表示否定意义，超过了需要的程度）

结构：too + 形容词/副词 + to do sth. 太……而不能……
The questions were too difficult for me. 这些题对我来说太难了。
He is too young to go to school. 他太小了，不能上学。

enough的用法：足够……（表示肯定意义，达到了需要的程度）

结构：形容词/副词 + enough + to do sth. 足够……可以做某事
The questions were easy enough for me. 这些题对我来说足够简单。
He is old enough to go to school. 他足够大了，可以上学了。

转换关系：

too + 形容词 + to do = not + 形容词反义词 + enough + to do
He is too young to go to school. = He is not old enough to go to school.
The box is too heavy for me to carry. = The box is not light enough for me to carry.

练习一：将下列直接引语改为间接引语

1. He said: "I like this book very much."
He said that ______ ______ ______ book very much.

2. She said: "I am writing a letter."
She said that ______ ______ ______ a letter.

3. Tom said: "I have already finished my homework."
Tom said that ______ ______ already ______ ______ homework.

4. He said: "I will go to Beijing tomorrow."
He said that ______ ______ go to Beijing ______ next day.

5. She said to me: "You can do it."
She told me that ______ ______ do it.

1. he liked that 2. she was writing 3. he had finished his 4. he would the 5. I could

练习二：用too或enough填空

1. The room is ______ small for us to live in.

2. He is old ______ to go to school.

3. The question is ______ difficult for me to answer.

4. The water is warm ______ for you to swim.

5. She ran ______ slowly to catch the bus.

1. too 2. enough 3. too 4. enough 5. too

3. The boy is ______ young to go to school.

4. The box is not ______ for me to carry.

5. He said he ______ to the party the next day.

1. B 2. B 3. B 4. B 5. C
