第109-112课 情态动词与名词数量表达

Lesson 109-112 | 核心句型：好主意、四便士一磅 | 语法点：情态动词should的用法、a little/a few的区别、much/many的用法、形容词和副词的最高级

核心句型

1. 第109-110课 好主意

Jane: What's the matter, Andy? 简：怎么了，安迪？
Andy: I'm making coffee. Do you want any? 安迪：我在煮咖啡，你要来点吗？
Jane: Yes, please. 简：好的，请来一点。
Andy: Do you want any sugar? 安迪：要放些糖吗？
Jane: Yes, please. 简：好的，谢谢。
Andy: Do you want any milk? 安迪：要加些牛奶吗？
Jane: No, thank you. I don't like milk in my coffee. I like black coffee. 简：不，谢谢。我不喜欢咖啡中放牛奶，我喜欢清咖啡。
Andy: Do you like biscuits? 安迪：你喜欢饼干吗？
Jane: Yes, I do. 简：是的，我喜欢。
Andy: Do you want one? 安迪：你要来一块吗？
Jane: Yes, please. 简：好的，请来一块。
Andy: Would you like some more? 安迪：你要不要再来点？
Jane: No, thank you. 简：不了，谢谢。
Andy: I think there are a few in that box. 安迪：我想那个盒子里还有一些。
Jane: I'm afraid it's empty. 简：恐怕盒子是空的。
Andy: What a pity! 安迪：真遗憾！
Jane: It doesn't matter. 简：没关系。
Andy: Go and have something to eat in the kitchen. 安迪：到厨房去吃点东西吧。
Jane: I'm going to make some tea. Would you like some? 简：我准备沏些茶，你要来点吗？
Andy: Yes, please. That's a good idea. 安迪：好的，谢谢。真是个好主意。

2. 第111-112课 四便士一磅

Tom: What are you doing, Carol? 汤姆：卡罗尔，你在干什么？
Carol: I'm making a shopping list, Tom. 卡罗尔：我在写购物单，汤姆。
Tom: What do we need? 汤姆：我们需要些什么？
Carol: We need a lot of things this week. 卡罗尔：这星期我们需要很多东西。
I must go to the grocer's. We haven't got much tea or coffee, and we haven't got any sugar or jam. 我得去一下食品店。我们的茶叶和咖啡不多了，糖和果酱也没有了。
Tom: What about vegetables? 汤姆：蔬菜呢？
Carol: I must go to the greengrocer's. We haven't got many tomatoes, but we've got a lot of potatoes. 卡罗尔：我得去一下蔬菜水果店。我们的西红柿不多了，但土豆还有许多。
I haven't got any oranges either. So I must get some. 我也没有橘子了，我得去买一些。
Tom: Have we got any beer and wine? 汤姆：我们还有啤酒和葡萄酒吗？
Carol: No, we haven't. And I'm not going to get any! 卡罗尔：没有了。不过我不打算去买！
Tom: I hope that you've got some money. 汤姆：我希望你还有钱。
Carol: I haven't got much. 卡罗尔：我的钱不多了。
Tom: Well, I haven't got much either! 汤姆：唉，我的也不多了！

语法知识点

1. 情态动词should的用法

should是情态动词，表示"应该、应当"，用来提出建议、劝告或表示义务，后接动词原形，没有人称和数的变化。

肯定句：主语 + should + 动词原形

You should see a doctor. 你应该去看医生。
We should study hard. 我们应该努力学习。
He should be more careful. 他应该更小心些。

否定句：主语 + should + not + 动词原形（缩写为shouldn't）

You shouldn't smoke here. 你不应该在这里抽烟。
We shouldn't waste time. 我们不应该浪费时间。
He shouldn't go to bed late. 他不应该晚睡。

疑问句：Should + 主语 + 动词原形？

Should I open the window? 我可以打开窗户吗？
Should we go now? 我们现在该走了吗？
Should he tell her the truth? 他应该告诉她真相吗？

2. a little和a few的用法

a little和a few都表示"一些，一点"，但用法不同：

表达 修饰名词 肯定含义 否定含义
a little 不可数名词 有一点（表示肯定） little 几乎没有（表示否定）
a few 可数名词复数 有几个（表示肯定） few 几乎没有（表示否定）

a little / little 修饰不可数名词：

There is a little milk in the bottle. 瓶子里还有一点牛奶。（肯定：有一些）
There is little milk in the bottle. 瓶子里几乎没有牛奶了。（否定：几乎没有）
I have a little money. 我有一点钱。（肯定：有一些）
I have little money. 我几乎没有钱了。（否定：几乎没有）

a few / few 修饰可数名词复数：

I have a few friends in Beijing. 我在北京有几个朋友。（肯定：有几个）
I have few friends in Beijing. 我在北京几乎没有朋友。（否定：几乎没有）
There are a few apples on the table. 桌子上有几个苹果。（肯定：有几个）
There are few apples on the table. 桌子上几乎没有苹果了。（否定：几乎没有）

3. much和many的用法

much和many都表示"许多"，但用法不同：

much 修饰不可数名词，多用于否定句和疑问句：

We haven't got much tea. 我们的茶叶不多了。
How much water do you want? 你想要多少水？
There isn't much time left. 剩下的时间不多了。

many 修饰可数名词复数，多用于否定句和疑问句：

We haven't got many tomatoes. 我们的西红柿不多了。
How many students are there in your class? 你们班有多少学生？
There aren't many people in the park today. 今天公园里人不多。

a lot of / lots of 既可以修饰可数名词也可以修饰不可数名词，多用于肯定句：

We have got a lot of potatoes. 我们有很多土豆。（可数名词）
We need a lot of money. 我们需要很多钱。（不可数名词）
There are lots of books in the library. 图书馆里有很多书。（可数名词）

4. 数量表达总结

可数名词复数 不可数名词
许多（肯定句） many / a lot of / lots of much / a lot of / lots of
许多（否定/疑问） many much
有一些（肯定） a few a little
几乎没有（否定） few little
提问数量 How many? How much?

5. 形容词和副词的最高级

最高级用于三者或三者以上的比较，表示"最……"，形容词最高级前面通常要加定冠词the，副词最高级前面的the可以省略。

单音节和部分双音节词：词尾加-est

small → smaller → the smallest
fast → faster → (the) fastest
happy → happier → the happiest
easy → easier → (the) easiest

多音节词和部分双音节词：前面加most

intelligent → more intelligent → the most intelligent
beautiful → more beautiful → the most beautiful
carefully → more carefully → (the) most carefully
expensive → more expensive → the most expensive

不规则变化：

good / well → better → the best
bad / badly → worse → the worst
many / much → more → the most
little → less → the least
far → farther / further → the farthest / the furthest

用法：

This is the best book I have ever read. 这是我读过的最好的书。
He runs (the) fastest in his class. 他是班上跑得最快的。
Shanghai is one of the biggest cities in China. 上海是中国最大的城市之一。

配套练习

练习一：用a little, a few, little, few填空

1. There is ______ water in the bottle. You can drink it.

2. He has ______ friends here, so he often feels lonely.

3. I have ______ apples. Would you like one?

4. There is ______ milk left. We need to buy some.

5. She has ______ CDs at home. She likes music very much.

1. a little 2. few 3. a few 4. little 5. a few

练习二：用much, many, a lot of填空

1. How ______ students are there in your school?

2. We don't have ______ time left.

3. There are ______ people in the park today.

4. How ______ does this book cost?

5. She has ______ friends and she often goes out with them.

1. many 2. much 3. a lot of 4. much 5. a lot of

练习三：用should或shouldn't填空

1. You ______ eat more vegetables. It's good for your health.

2. We ______ waste water. It's very precious.

3. ______ I tell him the truth?

4. You ______ drive so fast. It's dangerous.

5. He ______ see a doctor. He has a bad cold.

1. should 2. shouldn't 3. Should 4. shouldn't 5. should
