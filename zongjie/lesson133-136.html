第133-136课 宾语从句与现在完成时

Lesson 133-136 | 核心句型：激动人心的旅行、他说他是…… | 语法点：that引导的宾语从句、宾语从句中的时态呼应、现在完成时的被动语态

核心句型

1. 第133-134课 激动人心的旅行

Roy: I have just received a letter from my brother, Tim. He is in Australia. 罗伊：我刚刚收到弟弟蒂姆的来信，他现在在澳大利亚。
He has been there for six months. Tim is an engineer. He is working for a big firm and he has already visited a great number of different places in Australia. 他已经在那儿住了6个月了。蒂姆是个工程师，正在为一家大公司工作，并且已经去过澳大利亚的不少地方了。
He has just bought an Australian car and has gone to Alice Springs, a small town in the centre of Australia. He will soon visit Darwin. From there, he will fly to Perth. 他刚买了一辆澳大利亚小汽车，现在去了澳大利亚中部的小镇艾利斯斯普林斯。他不久还将到达尔文去，从那里，他再飞往珀斯。
My brother has never been abroad before, so he is finding this trip very exciting. 我弟弟以前从未出过国，因此，他觉得这次旅行非常激动人心。
I know him very well. He is a good student. He works very hard. He will be a good engineer. 我很了解他。他是个好学生。他学习很努力。他会成为一名优秀的工程师。

2. 第135-136课 他说他是……

Reporter: Have you just made a new film, Miss Marsh? 记者：马什小姐，您刚拍完一部新电影吗？
Miss Marsh: Yes, I have. 马什小姐：是的，我刚拍完。
Reporter: Are you going to make another? 记者：您准备再拍一部吗？
Miss Marsh: No, I'm not. I'm going to retire. I feel very tired. I don't want to make another film for a long time. 马什小姐：不，我不准备拍了。我准备退休了。我感到很累。我早就不想再拍电影了。
Reporter: Let's ask our reporter, Alan Jones. Alan, have you interviewed Miss Marsh? 记者：现在我们来采访一下我们的记者艾伦·琼斯。艾伦，你采访过马什小姐了吗？
Alan: Yes, I have. She said she had just made a new film. She said she was not going to make another. She said she was going to retire. She told me she felt very tired and she didn't want to make another film for a long time. 艾伦：是的，我采访过了。她说她刚拍完一部新电影。她说她不准备再拍了。她说她准备退休。她告诉我她感到很累，早就不想再拍电影了。
Reporter: Is that true, Miss Marsh? 记者：马什小姐，这是真的吗？
Miss Marsh: No, it isn't. 马什小姐：不，不是真的。
Miss Marsh: I said I was going to retire. But I changed my mind. I am going to make a new film next month. 马什小姐：我是说过我要退休。但我改变主意了。下个月我要拍一部新电影。
Reporter: Are you going to retire someday? 记者：您将来有一天会退休吗？
Miss Marsh: Yes, I am. But not now. I'm going to retire when I'm old. 马什小姐：是的，我会的。但不是现在。等我老了我就退休。

语法知识点

1. that引导的宾语从句

当宾语从句是陈述句时，用that引导，that在口语和非正式文体中可以省略。

基本结构：主句 + that + 陈述句

He said (that) he was very happy. 他说他很高兴。
I think (that) he will come tomorrow. 我认为他明天会来。
She told me (that) she had already finished her homework. 她告诉我她已经做完作业了。

常见的可以接that宾语从句的动词：

say, tell, think, know, believe, hope, hear, feel, suggest, agree等

I know that he is a good student. 我知道他是个好学生。
I believe that you can pass the exam. 我相信你能通过考试。
I hope that you will come to my birthday party. 我希望你能来参加我的生日聚会。

2. 宾语从句中的时态呼应

当主句的谓语动词是过去时态时，宾语从句的时态通常要作相应的变化，以保持和主句时态的一致。

直接引语（现在时态） 间接引语（过去时态） 变化规则
一般现在时 一般过去时 He said, "I am happy." → He said (that) he was happy.
现在进行时 过去进行时 He said, "I am reading." → He said (that) he was reading.
现在完成时 过去完成时 He said, "I have finished." → He said (that) he had finished.
一般过去时 过去完成时 He said, "I bought a book." → He said (that) he had bought a book.
一般将来时 过去将来时 He said, "I will come." → He said (that) he would come.
情态动词现在式 情态动词过去式 He said, "I can swim." → He said (that) he could swim.

特殊情况：如果从句表达的是客观真理、自然现象或习惯，即使主句是过去时，从句仍然用一般现在时：

The teacher told us that the earth moves around the sun. 老师告诉我们地球绕着太阳转。（客观真理，时态不变）
He said that he usually gets up at six o'clock. 他说他通常6点起床。（表示习惯，时态不变）

3. 现在完成时的被动语态

现在完成时的被动语态表示动作已经完成，且主语是动作的承受者。结构是"have/has + been + 过去分词"。

肯定句：主语 + have/has + been + 过去分词

The work has been finished. 工作已经完成了。
Many trees have been planted in the park. 公园里已经种了很多树。

否定句：主语 + have/has + not + been + 过去分词

The work has not been finished yet. 工作还没有完成。
The letter has not been posted. 信还没寄出去。

疑问句：Have/Has + 主语 + been + 过去分词？

Has the work been finished? 工作完成了吗？
Have the letters been posted? 信寄出去了吗？

使用场景：

不知道动作的执行者：My bike has been stolen. 我的自行车被偷了。
动作的执行者不重要：A new school has been built in our village. 我们村建了一所新学校。
强调动作的承受者：The book has been translated into many languages. 这本书已经被翻译成了多种语言。

4. have been to与have gone to的区别

短语 含义 用法 例句
have been to 去过某地（已经回来） 表示有过某种经历 I have been to Beijing three times. 我去过北京三次。（现在已经回来了）
have gone to 去了某地（还没回来） 表示现在在某地或在去某地的路上 He has gone to Beijing. He will be back next week. 他去北京了，下周回来。（现在不在这里）

have been in表示在某地待了多长时间：

He has been in Shanghai for ten years. 他在上海已经待了十年了。

5. a number of与the number of的区别

短语 含义 谓语动词 例句
a number of 许多，大量 复数 A number of students are playing on the playground. 许多学生在操场上玩。
the number of ……的数量 单数 The number of students in our class is 50. 我们班学生的数量是50人。

6. 过去将来时

过去将来时表示从过去的某个时间看将要发生的动作或存在的状态，由"would + 动词原形"或"was/were going to + 动词原形"构成。

would + 动词原形：

He said he would come tomorrow. 他说他明天会来。
I knew she would help us. 我知道她会帮助我们的。

was/were going to + 动词原形：

She said she was going to buy a new bike. 她说她打算买一辆新自行车。
They told me they were going to have a party. 他们告诉我他们打算举办一个聚会。

过去将来时常用于宾语从句中，主句是过去时态：

I thought he would attend the meeting. 我原以为他会参加会议的。
He said he wasn't going to go with us. 他说他不打算和我们一起去。

配套练习

练习一：将下列直接引语改为间接引语

1. He said, "I am very busy now."

2. She said, "I will go to the park tomorrow."

3. They said, "We have finished our homework."

4. He told me, "I bought a new car last week."

5. She said, "I can speak three languages."

1. He said (that) he was very busy then.

2. She said (that) she would go to the park the next day.

3. They said (that) they had finished their homework.

4. He told me (that) he had bought a new car the week before.

5. She said (that) she could speak three languages.

练习二：用have been to, have gone to或have been in填空

1. He ______ Beijing three times. He knows the city very well.

2. Where is Tom? He ______ the library. He will be back in an hour.

3. My father ______ Shanghai for two months. He will come back next week.

4. ______ you ever ______ Sydney?

5. How long ______ you ______ this city?

1. has been to 2. has gone to 3. has been in 4. Have been to 5. have been in

练习三：将下列句子改为现在完成时的被动语态

1. We have finished the work.

2. They have built three new schools in the town.

3. Someone has stolen my bike.

4. I have already posted the letters.

5. The workers have repaired the car.

1. The work has been finished.

2. Three new schools have been built in the town.

3. My bike has been stolen.

4. The letters have already been posted.

5. The car has been repaired by the workers.

练习四：选择填空

1. He said he ______ to the party the next day.

2. ______ students in our school are from the countryside.
A. A number of B. The number of C. The number

3. The work ______ yet.
A. has finished B. hasn't been finished C. hasn't finished

4. He ______ London. He will be back next month.
A. has been to B. has gone to C. has been in

5. I know ______ he is a good student.

1. B 2. A 3. B 4. B 5. B

练习五：翻译下列句子

1. 他告诉我他已经写完作业了。

2. 我去过长城三次。

3. 这本书已经被翻译成多种语言了。

4. 她说她下周会来看我。

5. 我们学校的学生数量是2000人。

1. He told me (that) he had already finished his homework.

2. I have been to the Great Wall three times.

3. This book has been translated into many languages.

4. She said (that) she would come to see me next week.
